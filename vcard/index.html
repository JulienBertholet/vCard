Julien Bertholet is a student in web design and unlike Jules Cesar, he doesn’t refer to himself in the third person ...

Im’ from Brussels and now I live in Namur for my studies. I like to travel, explore and take great photos. I daily follow the spirit of martial arts, wich I practice every week for quite a time.  

Every design is like a fight. You don’t have to punch your PC, but you always have to try different approaches. In both cases you have to push your limits to give the best of what you can bring  .

I see the web like I see the earth : full of great landscapes that all have something to offer you. I want to make it look like Planet Earth by BBC, not like a silly Moon.
